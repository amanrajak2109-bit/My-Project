The Ramayan (Ramayana) is one of the greatest and most important epics of ancient India.
It was written by the sage Valmiki and is traditionally dated to around 500 BCE to 100 BCE.


    This is an order list

  1. Rama is exiled to the forest for 14 years.
  2. His wife Sita and brother Lakshmana go with him.
  3. The demon king Ravana kidnaps Sita and takes her to Lanka.
  4. With the help of Hanuman and an army of monkeys, Rama fights Ravana.
  5. Rama defeats Ravana and rescues Sita.
  6. After completing his exile, Rama returns to Ayodhya and becomes king.
